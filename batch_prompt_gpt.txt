You must be conservative with BUY ratings. You are scoring probability, not upside.

Objective:
For each ticker packet, estimate whether it has >60% probability of achieving:
A) +10% within the next 7–10 trading days (short horizon)
B) +10% within the next 30 calendar days (medium horizon)

You MUST produce two separate scores and verdicts (Short + Medium), then provide a combined ranking.
You may select at most:
- 1 BUY for the 7–10 day horizon
- 2 BUY for the 30 day horizon
All other ratings must be WATCH or IGNORE.

Hard Constraints (anti-hallucination):
1) You MUST NOT invent catalysts. If earnings date is Unknown or headlines are missing, you MUST penalize it.

Horizon B: 30 Calendar Days Score (0–100)
